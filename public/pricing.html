Pricing FAQs

Is there a free plan?

Yes, our Free plan includes basic grant search, up to 5 AI matches per month, and access to our grant database. No credit card required.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, all plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. Cancel anytime from your account settings.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) through Stripe. Enterprise plans can pay by invoice.

What is the difference between SME and Professional?

Professional adds AI match explanations, priority matching for new grants, and PDF/CSV export reports.

How many client orgs can Consultants manage?

The Consultant plan supports up to 10 client organizations. Need more? Contact us about Enterprise pricing.

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes, annual billing saves you 20% compared to monthly pricing. Contact our sales team to set up annual billing.

What does Enterprise include?

Full API access, unlimited organizations, custom integrations, dedicated account manager, SLA guarantees, and priority support.

Can I upgrade or downgrade?

Yes, change your plan anytime. Upgrades are immediate with prorated billing. Downgrades take effect next billing cycle.
