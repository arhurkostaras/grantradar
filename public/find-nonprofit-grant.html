Nonprofit Grant FAQs

What types of grants are available for nonprofits?

Nonprofits can access foundation grants, government program funding, corporate giving programs, community development grants, and international aid funding.

Do I need 501(c)(3) status to apply?

In the USA, most grants require 501(c)(3) status. However, fiscal sponsorship can allow organizations without tax-exempt status to apply. Outside the USA, equivalent charitable registration is typically required.

How do I find grants that match my mission?

GrantRadar uses AI to match your nonprofit's mission, programs, and geographic focus with relevant funding opportunities. Create a profile and we will score and rank grants by fit.

What is the typical grant size for nonprofits?

Nonprofit grants range from $1,000 community grants to multi-million dollar foundation program grants. Most fall between $10,000 and $250,000.

Can nonprofits apply for government grants?

Yes, many government grant programs specifically target nonprofits for delivering social services, community development, arts, healthcare, and education programs.
