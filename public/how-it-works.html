How GrantRadar Works

Four simple steps from profile to funding. Our AI does the heavy lifting so you can focus on what matters.

1

Build Your Organization Profile

Tell us about your organization in under 5 minutes. We need to know your:

  • Country and location
  • Organization type (SME, startup, nonprofit, research)
  • Industry sectors
  • Size, revenue, and founding year
  • Special qualifiers (Indigenous, women-owned, nonprofit status)
2

AI Scans 10,000+ Grants Daily

Our AI engine continuously crawls and indexes grant programs from around the world. It checks:

  • Eligibility criteria against your profile
  • Sector and industry alignment
  • Geographic requirements
  • Organization size and type requirements
  • Funding amount appropriateness
3

Get Scored and Ranked Matches

Every grant is scored against your profile on a 0-100% scale. You see:

  • Match percentage showing how well you fit
  • Match reasons (what criteria you meet)
  • Funding range and deadline countdown
  • Email alerts when new matches appear
4

Apply With Confidence

We give you everything you need to submit winning applications:

  • Direct links to application portals
  • Deadline tracking with reminders
  • Application status tracking
  • Success rate analytics
